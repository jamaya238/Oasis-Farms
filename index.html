Who We Are

Founded in 2025 in Howard County, Maryland, Oasis Farms is on a mission to help our community “live full.” Starting with microgreens and now focusing on vibrant, pesticide-free basil, we grow fresh, high-quality produce year-round using sustainable hydroponic systems. Our commitment is simple: every customer deserves food at its peak freshness.

What We Do

At Oasis Farms, we cultivate premium hydroponic basil and have experience growing microgreens. Our indoor systems maximize plant health, flavor, and growth efficiency. We deliver directly to homes, restaurants, and retailers across Baltimore, DC, and Virginia, ensuring produce arrives fresh and ready to use. Growing exceptional food is just the start, we aim to feed communities, one full belly at a time.
